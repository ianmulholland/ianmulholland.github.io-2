SEWA Bharat Community Center Case Management System

SEWA Bharat is a grassroots NGO that services over a million women informal sector workers in India. While working at SEWA, I built a tool that helped community center workers process applications for welfare and government schemes. This data was used to create transparency and accountability for local government offices responsible for distrubting benefits and as a tool to help track the needs of communities across 50,000 users in Delhi and Bengal.
